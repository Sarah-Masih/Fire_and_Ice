The end of the world has been predicted to happen in many ways. The most popular ways are through war which causes humanity to be destroyed, or through a great Ice Age.
Many have realised that in case a world war 3 were to begin, it would end up with major countries fighting with nuclear weapons that will end all humanity.
It would be difficult to survive in a post apocalypse resulting from such a war since it will cause economies to crash and drive billions into homelessness.
It will cause the world to take extreme measures to end the war and bring back peace and the world as we know it may cease to exist.
