Ice

The second most popular way the world can end is through a very long and hard winter.
This one is more probable considering the peace treaties signed by nearly all countries across the globe.
If humanity were to survive until the Earth shifts into the next ice age, it would be faced with a brutal cold across the entire globe and excessive food shortage due to loss of agricultural land.
Resources gained through mining would become nearly impossible to extract because the ground would be frozen, the water level would drop drastically.
However, this would take thousands of years to happen, and we are lucky enough to be part of a timeline in which the last ice age has not completely ended yet.
