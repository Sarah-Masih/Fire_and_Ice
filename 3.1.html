Desire

Humans have a desire for many things in life.
A desire to be loved, understood or even to have fun. So how could desire end up related to the end of the world? We must understand the human desire to cause destruction and how it brings us joy.
While this may sound haunting or even sinister, it is referencing to something quite innocent, such as dominoes or a game of Jenga.
Paintball is also a harmless way to feed this desire of destruction without causing real harm.
The joy of popping a balloon during a party or a shoving a pyramid of playing cards are also another example.
However, when this desire of destruction meets hate between communities, it is capable of starting wars using weapons intended for harm.
