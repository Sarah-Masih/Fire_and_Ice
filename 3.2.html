Hate

Hate exists in humanity and is often learnt through generational upbringing; however, it is possible for it to disappear and communities to be acceptable for everyone.
The world understands the consequences of war and has taken measures to avoid it such as forming United Nations.
However, hate causes communities to divide and avoid each other, this may end up causing the world to end up with a cold war, where weapons are not involved, but there is a constant threat and fear of another community.
Communications would cease, economies would fall, and countries may close their borders.
