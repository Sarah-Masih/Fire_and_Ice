Loss

The loss of a living being/thing is what makes the end of the world as we know it, truly the end of our life.
It is what comes during and after the end of the world.
It can also be the loss of an era, such as an era when electronics did not exist yet.
It can be the loss of a language that is never spoken or a culture forgotten.
Loss can be many things, but in the end it is shaped by how we handle it.
