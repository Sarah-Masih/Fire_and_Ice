Perseverence

Perseverance is what comes after facing the end of the world.
When the wounds are healed, when the changes are accepted.
It is what keeps us going. When winter ends, and spring begins, it feels as if the world has come back from the dead.
If humanity was to suffer an ice age, it would take a lot of energy, and will power to continue surviving and break through.
But hopefully by then, technology would be improved enough that we can comfortably get through the harsh winters or begin a civilization somewhere else in the universe.
